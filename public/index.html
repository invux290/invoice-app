Frequently asked questions

Is QwikBill really free? ▼

Yes. The core invoice generator is completely free with no limits. You can create and download as many invoices as you want without signing up or entering a credit card.

Do I need to create an account? ▼

No account needed. Open the app, fill in your details, and download your PDF. Your business details are saved locally in your browser for next time.

Is my data safe and private? ▼

Your invoice data is never stored on our servers. The PDF is generated and sent directly to your browser. Nothing is saved, logged, or shared with anyone.

Can I add GST to my invoice? ▼

Yes. There is a tax rate field where you can enter 18 for 18% GST (or any other rate). The tax amount and final total are calculated automatically and shown on the PDF.

What currencies are supported? ▼

INR (₹), USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), CAD and AUD. Select your currency from the dropdown and the correct symbol appears on the invoice automatically.
